Yippee Noodles

Ingredients

  • Noodle
  • water
  • vegetables
  • vegitable oil
  • egg
  • cheese
  • soya sauce
  • chilli sauce

    • Preparation

  • Boil water in a container and add the noodle and cook for 3 minutes.
  • when cooked take it,drain it
  • Take a pan put some oil, add the vegetables and cook it.
  • when the vegtables are cooked, add 2 eggs and scramble it
  • Now add the noodles that we have kept aside, add some soya and chilli sauce
  • Finally add somme cheese and mix well
  • your noodles is ready to serve
